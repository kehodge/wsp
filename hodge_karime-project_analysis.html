Site Intent

The redesign objective of Get Gaming Orlando is to make their website pages more visually appealing to captivate users and increase traffic to website. Also the number of pages contain in the site need to be reduce simple because the user wants a site that they can navigate clearly and understand and not get confused and frustrated by unnecessary pages. Condense pages from 7 to 4 by eliminating hello friend, coupons, and FAQ's pages and consolidate them into the remaining 4 pages.

By restructuring their website structure and pages it will make it more visually ecstatic and will attract more visitors and potentially more customers the to website.

Competitive Analysis

Because of the the competition in Orlando from many large and small companies in the video game consoles and computer repair market, focusing on your up to date servicing repair, your low and affordable prices and standing out above the rest will help to bring in more potential customers for their electronics needs.

Targeted Audience

Our targeted audience are male teenagers to young adults (13 to 24) and small business owners. Our Male audience we are targeting makes between $0 and $35,000 are living with their parents, attending school and working part-time. Because money will not be abundant to purchase a new video game console or computer between these ages you have a better chance having this audience come in for repairs. Small business owners who are start ups or are save money, this group will be the second targeted audience.
